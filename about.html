Diana’s Journey: Turning Pain into Purpose

My name is Diana, and I founded *Breaking Barriers* to amplify the voices often silenced — girls forced into early marriages, women navigating stigma, and communities fighting for dignity.

As a girl growing up in Sierra Leone, I witnessed the harm caused by silence and tradition. Today, I use my story, and the stories of many others, to create change. This platform is not just mine — it’s ours.

Let’s rise together.

“Every time we break the silence, we break the barrier.”
